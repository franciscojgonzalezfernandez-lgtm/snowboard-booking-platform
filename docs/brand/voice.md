# The Drop — Voice & Copy Guide

> Territory: **"Your coach"** (personal), chosen with the owner 2026-06-15. Canonical brand
> summary: PRD §2.4. Visual tokens: `tokens.md`. Motion: `motion.md`. Logo: `logo-assets.md`.
> Ticket: **F-105**.

## Who's talking

Javi — one rider who coaches. Not a school, not a brand committee. Every line should sound like
him talking to you on the chairlift.

## The voice in one line

A great rider who happens to be a good coach: **warm, direct, first-person, allergic to hype.**

## Six principles

1. **First person, always.** "I'll get you riding," never "Our instructors will." The product is a person.
   - Do: *"I ride here every week. I'll show you where the good snow is."*
   - Don't: *"The Drop offers premium coaching experiences."*
2. **Beat the fear, don't ignore it.** Name what scares people, then dissolve it.
   - Do: *"Nervous about the steeper stuff? That's exactly what an hour with me is for."*
   - Don't: *"Suitable for all ability levels."*
3. **Short. Concrete. Earned.** Real specifics (Flumserberg, the COLORS door, your video on your phone)
   carry the weight — not adjectives.
   - Do: *"Two hours. We meet at the COLORS door. You leave riding blue runs."*
   - Don't: *"An unforgettable journey of progression."*
4. **Rider, not brochure.** Use the words riders actually use. Never corporate.
5. **Confident, never loud.** No exclamation marks as a crutch. The calm of someone who knows.
6. **Honest about the deal.** The lift pass and gear aren't included — say so plainly, then help.
   The honesty *is* the premium.

## By surface (examples)

- **Hero:** *"I'm Javi. I'll take you from first turns to first drops."*
- **Pricing card — 1h** (kicker *Confidence*): *"Fix one thing, or get your nerve back. One hour, just us."*
- **Pricing card — 4h** (kicker *Go Deep*): *"Freestyle or carving — four hours to actually change how you ride. Video while we go, and on your phone after."*
- **CTA:** *"Book your lesson"* · *"See the classes"* (never "Buy now")
- **Availability empty:** *"Nothing free that day — here are the closest dates I can ride with you."*
- **Error:** *"That didn't go through. Try again, or just call me: +41 76 638 18 70."*
- **Post-class email:** *"Good session today. Here's your video — watch the toe-edge bit at 0:42."*
- **FAQ — lift pass:** *"No, you buy your own day pass. I'll point you to the right counter."*

## Per locale (adapt tone — never translate literally)

- **EN** — the master voice. Relaxed, confident, a little dry.
- **DE** — informal **du**, warm not stiff; Swiss spelling (**ss**, never ß). Slightly more precise, still human.
- **ES** — informal **tú**, close and warm; energetic without losing the calm-expert core.

## Glossary — say this

rider · ride · session · line · drop in · toe/heel edge · carve · butter · first tracks ·
blue/red run · the north (of Switzerland) · the COLORS door

## Forbidden

"experience" (as a noun) · "journey" · "unleash" · "elevate" · "premium" (show it, don't say it) ·
"world-class" · "passionate team" · exclamation-as-hype · emoji · literal translations · talking
about Javi in the third person.

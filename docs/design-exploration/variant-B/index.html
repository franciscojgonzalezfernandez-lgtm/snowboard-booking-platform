§ 01 — Built for serious learners
01 / Pairing

One instructor.
For the whole day.

No swapping between groups. You pair with a single instructor for the entire lesson — they learn how you ride and stay with you.

02 / Sizes

Groups of 1 to 4.
Friends or family.

All lessons are private. Bring up to three companions; same price up to four heads. We never combine bookings.

03 / Languages

English. Deutsch.
Español.

Pick your instructor by the language you ride in. We match you to someone who teaches in your tongue, not just translates.
